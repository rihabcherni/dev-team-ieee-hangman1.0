Introduction

Welcome to the Hangman Game, a classic word-guessing game with a twist. In this book, we'll guide you through the game's rules, how to play, and some tips and tricks.

3

Game Rules

In Hangman, you need to guess the letters of a hidden word. Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman figure. If the full figure is completed before you guess the word, you lose!

4

How to Play

  1. Select a difficulty level.
  2. Start guessing letters.
  3. Win by guessing the word before the figure is completed!
